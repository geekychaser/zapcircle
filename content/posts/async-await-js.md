ECMAScript 2017 brought in syntactic sugar on top of `Promises` in JavaScript in form of `async` and `await`. Async/await provides a special syntax to work with promises in a more comfortable fashion. They  allow us to write asynchronous code in synchronous manner.

<!--more-->

Before proceeing with __aysnc/await__ you need to understand __Promises__. You can head over to [Promises in JS](https://zapcircle.net/posts/promise-js/) article to read and learn about promises.
